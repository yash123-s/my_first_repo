CLIENT

According to its 2017 annual shareholder report, Trip advisor earns 43% of its revenues through the subsidiaries of its two most significant advertising partners, Expedia (its former parent company) and Priceline Group (now called Booking Holdings). It derives a substantial portion of its revenue from advertising and redirections. These include revenues from the sale of advertising, mostly through click-based advertising and, to a lesser extent, display-based and subscription-based advertising. The company also has master advertising contracts with its advertising partners.The website services are free to users, who provide most of the content, and the website is supported by a hotel booking facility and an advertising business model.
